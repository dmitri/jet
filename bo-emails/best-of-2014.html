Berklee Online's Best of 2014

2014 was the biggest year yet for Berklee's online students and community. Whether they’re performing at the Grammy Awards or receiving accolades for their teaching methods, our 30,000 students and faculty members from over 150 countries continue to achieve greatness year after year. Here’s to a wonderful 2015!

 
Biggest Student Story:
Andrew Joslyn Tours with Macklemore
 
Online certificate student, composer, and multi-instrumentalist Andrew began the year on tour with superstars Macklemore & Ryan Lewis, even performing with the chart-topping duo -- along with Madonna! -- at the 2014 Grammy Awards. Meet Andrew
 
Most Acclaimed Instructor:
Brad Hatfield Wins Excellence in Teaching Award
 
Brad won big this year, receiving the 2014 UPCEA Excellence in Teaching Award for his online course, Music Supervision. Meet Brad
 
Most Popular Degree Major:
Music Production Attracts Thousands of Applicants
 
We launched our first online bachelor’s degree program this year and welcomed our inaugural class in September. Apply Now
 
Biggest MOOC:
Coursera Guitar Course Reaches Over 300,000 Online Students
 
Thaddeus Hogarth reached thousands of students in our free massive open online course Introduction to Guitar through Coursera. Sign Up Now
 
Newest Course:
